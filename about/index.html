Bloomly started from a simple need: a place to be honest.

Many young people carry stress, loneliness, and fear without knowing where to put it. Bloomly gives them a calm place to read, reflect, and ask for support.

What Bloomly stands for

These values guide the writing, the Charla sessions, and every product decision.

💜

Honesty

We do not pretend hard days are easy. We name what is real and handle it with care.

🌍

Accessibility

Support should not feel out of reach. Bloomly keeps language, pricing, and tools simple.

🤝

Community

People need room to speak without being judged. Bloomly makes that room quieter and safer.

The person building Bloomly

Bloomly is led by Manuel Muhunami.

Want to be a Charla counsellor or mentor?

We're building a small, trusted team of counsellors and peer mentors who genuinely care about young people's wellbeing. If you're a trained counsellor, psychologist, or mental health professional based in Kenya — or a university student in a psychology-related field looking to gain supervised experience — we'd love to hear from you. Sessions are conducted online, at times that work for both you and the client. We handle all bookings, payments, and matching. You focus on the conversation. Fill in the form below to express your interest. We review applications on a rolling basis and will be in touch within 7 days.

My Story

Why I Built Bloomly

By Manuel Muhunami · Founder & Lead Builder

Bloomly started with one belief: young people should have a calm place where honesty is allowed.

I built Bloomly to stay practical and human. The blog gives language to quiet feelings. Charla gives people a way to speak with someone when they need support.

The platform is still growing, but the purpose is steady: make support easier to reach and easier to trust.
