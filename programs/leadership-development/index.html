Leadership

Leadership Development Program

Training students in leadership, communication, and initiative through meaningful real-world projects.

Join Interest ListExplore more programs

Program overview

A project-based experience where students practice leading with empathy, clarity, and action.

Who this is for

Students who want to build confidence, communicate better, and start initiatives in their school or community.

What students gain

  • Communication and facilitation practice
  • Project planning skills
  • Confidence to take initiative

Weekly structure

  1. Week 1: Leadership style and purpose
  2. Week 2: Communication and teamwork
  3. Week 3: Design a small initiative
  4. Week 4: Share, reflect, and plan next steps
