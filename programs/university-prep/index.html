Program overview

A guided pathway for students preparing applications with clarity, confidence, and a stronger story.

Who this is for

Students planning for university, scholarships, essays, extracurricular profiles, or future pathways after school.

What students gain

  • Application timeline and checklist
  • Personal statement direction
  • Profile and scholarship readiness

Weekly structure

  1. Week 1: Goals and application map
  2. Week 2: Story mining and essay prompts
  3. Week 3: Activities and profile building
  4. Week 4: Review plan and next submissions
