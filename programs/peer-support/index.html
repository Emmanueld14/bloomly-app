Peer Support

Bloomly Peer Support Program

A structured peer-to-peer mentorship and emotional support network for students who want safe connection and consistent encouragement.

Join Interest List Explore more programs

Program overview

This program helps students feel seen through guided peer conversations, mentorship check-ins, and clear boundaries for emotional safety.

Who this is for

Students who want a calm space to talk, listen, build confidence, and feel less alone during school or personal transitions.

What students gain

  • Supportive peer connection
  • Healthy listening and boundary skills
  • Confidence to ask for help early

Weekly structure

  1. Week 1: Safety, trust, and group agreements
  2. Week 2: Listening without judgment
  3. Week 3: Sharing stress and coping tools
  4. Week 4: Reflection, next steps, and support map
