Questions

Before you join

Clear answers help students feel safe before taking the first step.

Bloomly programs are designed for students and young people who want support, growth, and practical guidance in a safe environment.

Some programs can be free, while guided cohorts or partner workshops may have a small fee. Each program page will clearly show the format before sign-up.

Most Bloomly programs can run online first, with school or partner sessions added where safe and appropriate.

Students, mentors, counsellors, or schools can suggest an initiative. Bloomly can help shape the goal, rhythm, resources, and safety guidelines.

Peer supporters should receive guidance on listening, boundaries, confidentiality, escalation, and when to involve a trusted adult or professional support.

Host with Bloomly
