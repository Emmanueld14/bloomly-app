Mental Wellness

Mental Wellness Circle

Safe group sessions focused on emotional wellbeing, stress management, resilience, and everyday coping tools.

Join Interest ListExplore more programs

Program overview

A calm group circle for learning wellness tools, naming stress, and practicing supportive reflection.

Who this is for

Students who feel emotionally tired, stressed, isolated, or curious about gentle self-care habits.

What students gain

  • Stress management tools
  • Emotional language and reflection
  • Supportive group connection

Weekly structure

  1. Week 1: Creating emotional safety
  2. Week 2: Understanding stress signals
  3. Week 3: Mindfulness, journaling, and resets
  4. Week 4: Personal resilience plan
