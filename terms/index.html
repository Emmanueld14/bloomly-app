Terms & Conditions

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who we are

Bloomly (bloomly.co.ke) is a mental wellness platform for young people in Kenya, operated by Manuel Muhunami.

2. Charla Sessions

Charla sessions are peer support or counselling conversations. They are not a substitute for clinical mental health treatment or emergency services.

3. Privacy

We do not share your data with third parties. Your name and email are stored securely in our database and used only to send you Bloomly content.

4. Payments

Payments made via M-Pesa or Stripe are non-refundable once a session is confirmed.

5. Crisis Support

Bloomly is not an emergency service. If you are in crisis, contact Befrienders Kenya: 0800 723 253 (free, 24/7) or visit befrienderskenya.org.

6. Changes

We may update these terms. Continued use of the site means you accept the updated terms.
