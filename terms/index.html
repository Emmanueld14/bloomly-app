Last updated: May 8, 2026

1. About Bloomly

Bloomly is a mental wellness website for teens and young people. It publishes reflections, hosts comments where available, accepts newsletter signups, and offers paid Charla sessions for private support conversations.

Bloomly is not an emergency service. If you are in immediate danger or may harm yourself or someone else, contact local emergency services or a crisis support line right away.

2. Using the platform

You may use Bloomly to read content, subscribe to updates, comment where comments are available, and book Charla sessions. You agree to use the site respectfully and lawfully.

You must not use Bloomly to harass others, post abusive content, impersonate another person, upload harmful code, attempt to break the site, or interfere with another user's access.

3. Charla sessions

Charla sessions are private support conversations booked through the Bloomly appointment flow and scheduled through Calendly after payment is confirmed.

Charla sessions are meant for reflection, listening, and practical support. They are not medical treatment, psychiatric care, emergency response, or a guaranteed outcome.

4. Payments

Bloomly accepts supported payment methods shown at checkout. Kenyan users may use M-Pesa where available. Non-Kenyan users may use card payment through Stripe Checkout.

A booking is not confirmed until payment is successfully completed and the Calendly booking step is finished. If payment is pending, the booking remains pending.

5. Refunds, cancellations, and missed sessions

If you need to cancel or reschedule, use the Calendly link in your confirmation email as early as possible.

Refund requests are reviewed case by case. Bloomly may decline a refund when a session has already taken place, when a user misses a session without notice, or when payment provider records show the service was delivered.

If Bloomly cancels a paid session, the user may choose a new available slot or request a refund for that session.

User content includes comments, blog submissions, messages, names, and any other material a user sends to Bloomly.

You are responsible for the content you submit. Do not submit content that is abusive, false, unlawful, invasive of another person's privacy, or owned by someone else without permission.

Bloomly may moderate, remove, or decline to publish user content when needed to protect users, keep discussions safe, or comply with the law.

Bloomly collects only the information needed to run the site, deliver email updates, process bookings, handle payments, and keep the service safe. This may include your name, email address, phone number for mobile payments, booking details, payment status, comments, and basic technical data.

Payments are processed through third-party providers such as M-Pesa and Stripe. Bloomly does not store card numbers.

Bloomly may use trusted service providers for hosting, analytics, email delivery, authentication, bookings, and payments. Those providers process data needed for their service.
